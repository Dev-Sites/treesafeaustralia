Wildlife relocation & nesting box installation.

We employ wildlife spotters/catchers to assist us in wildlife relocation, habitat recognition and placement of nesting boxes in applicable trees. Our arborist reports mention that wildlife inspections are to occur before any vegetation disturbance and relocation of any such flora or fauna is implemented. We take great pride in performing these works, as caring for our native wildlife is paramount to the existence of these species. We can provide flora & fauna reports as well as the rescue of any such creature.

A remarkable success story, this Osprey nest was relocated from a development site. By the next nesting season, the adult Ospreys were back preparing their nest for the next mating season.

Our Promise To You

We stand by our trusted reputation of quality and professionalism.
Are you insured?

Yes, we have all Certificates of Currency and will happily send you copies if you need them.

What qualifications do your team have?

Most of our crew have a Cert III in Arboriculture. All have appropriate licenses to operate the machinery and equipment we use.

We also have fully qualified Level 5 Arborists that can consult, assess, and provide compliancy reports if required. 

Do we get to keep the mulch?

It is yours. You may keep it. We will leave it in a pile for you.

Will my garden be damaged?

Sometimes damage does occur when branches fall from heights but we will do our best to prevent this.

Do I have to clean up after the job is finished?

No, we do a very good job of making sure the area is left clean.

How long does a quote stay current?

We review all quotes after 60 days.

Can you do Safety Audits for trees?

Yes, we have level 5 Arborists to do this.

What if I am not happy with the outcome?

We do our best to make sure everyone understands what works have been agreed upon. If you are not happy we do ask that you tell us (preferable while the crew is there). We aim to exceed your expectations.

What if I need Council approvals?

We can do this for you for a fee or we can advise you on the process so you can do it yourself.

Do I have to pay on the day?

No, you pay after the works are completed and you are happy with the outcome. A 7 day invoice will be emailed to you. This can be paid by cash, cheque, or EFT.
