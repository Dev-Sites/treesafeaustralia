Our Promise To You

We stand by our trusted reputation of quality and professionalism.
Are you insured?

We hold a WorkCover Insurance policy in accordance with the Workplace Injury Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 2013 (and amendments).

We also have General & Products Liability Insurance which covers any potential third party damage. This means both our workers, and your site, are fully insured should anything go wrong during a job.

Do you work with council permits?

Yes. We have intimate knowledge of obtaining council permits. We understand each council within Queensland has a set of regulations for which trees can be removed and pruned. Drawing on our considerable horticultural and arboricultural knowledge, we can identify tree species that fall within these regulations and consult you for further action.

Do you respond to emergency call-outs?

Yes. We are quick and responsive to emergency call-outs. If someone reports an issue of a fallen or dangerous tree, we can attend the site and ensure the tree is no longer an immediate threat to the public. We'll give you a quote, and we can return and take appropriate action for the tree at an arranged time.

What is the cost of tree removal on council land?

The cost of your tree removal will vary depending on where it is located, how easy it is for us to access, its size and several other factors. If you would like a free tree inspection, contact us today, and we can give you a more detailed quote.

Do you provide services on private property?

We offer residential and commercial services for pruning and removing trees and a wide variety of tree services, from cable bracing to arborist reports. We find it helpful to work in private and public sectors as sometimes private trees fall on council land and vice versa.

Being well-versed in the current regulations and laws protecting trees on both public and private land allows us to provide you with superior service and help with all your tree care concerns.

How long does a quote stay current?

We review all quotes after 60 days.

Can you do Safety Audits for trees?

Yes, we have level 5 Arborists to do this.

Are your arborist reports certified?

Our team of Arborists hold the appropriate certificates in arboriculture to provide comprehensive arborist reports following the relevant Australian standards. We‚Äôll conduct inspections to assess the site and perform a Hazard and Risk Assessment of your trees.

What if I need Council approvals?

We can do this for you for a fee or we can advise you on the process so you can do it yourself.

Do you remove tree roots and stumps?

We often recommend stump grinding when we remove a tree as we believe it provides the best results with the least disruption to the surrounding vegetation and wildlife.
